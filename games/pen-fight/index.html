How to Play

  • Move your mouse over the arena to aim
  • Adjust power with the slider
  • Click the arena or press Flick to launch your pen
  • Knock your opponent's pen off the desk to score
  • If your own pen falls off, opponent scores
  • First to 3 points wins!
